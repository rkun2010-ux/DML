### Double machine learning simulation results ($n=1000$, 200 replications).

Survival and censoring times are generated via AFT models: $\log T_i = X_i^{\top} \beta\_0 + \epsilon\_i$ and $\log C\_i = \cos(2\pi X\_{i1}) + 1 / (1 + \exp(-X_{i2} - X_{i2})) + X_{i4} - 1.23 + \epsilon'\_i$. The covariates are $X\_{ij} \sim \mathcal{U}(0, 1)$ for $j=1, \dots, 4$. 
The true coefficient vectors are $\beta\_0 = (-0.5, 0.5, 0.5, -0.5)^{\top}$, with random errors $\epsilon\_i \sim \mathcal{N}(0, 0.5^2)$ and $\epsilon'\_i \sim \mathcal{N}(0,2^2)$.
